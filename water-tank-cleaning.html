Why are water tank cleaning services so important in U.A.E.?

Unlike many countries which have the benefit of water direct to tap, properties in Dubai receive water through a tank and pump system. Unfortunately, dirt, debris and other contaminants can build up in water tanks over time, affecting the quality of your water They also make your water tank the perfect breeding ground for bacteria, and it's not unusual to find Legionella and other water-borne diseases lurking within them. This is just one reason why Dubai Municipality recommends water tank cleaning every six months.

Our Water Tank Cleaning Process

Our comprehensive 6-step water tank cleaning process ensures the highest standards of cleanliness and safety for your water supply.

1
TANK INSPECTION

A preliminary inspection of the tank and implementation of all necessary pre-cleaning measures and tool organisation.

2
PRESSURE WASHER & MECHANICAL CLEANING

Comprehensive removal of dirt using a powerful pressure washer with hand tools utilised for detail areas.

3
TANK DISINFECTION

Sanitisation of the internal surface of the water tank using Dubai Municipality-approved chemicals. This eliminates all contaminants.

4
REMOVAL OF CONTAMINANTS USING A SUBMERSIBLE PUMP

Extensive rinsing, collection and draining of dirty water with a submersible pump.

5
REVIEW OF SERVICES PROVIDED

A visual review of the services provided with before/after pictures of the clean tank shared with the customer.

6
VERIFICATION OF THE CLEANING & DISINFECTION PROCESS

After the cleaning and disinfection process, water samples will be collected in sterilised bottles by trained sample collector aseptically and analysed in any of the EIAC approved laboratories to check the effectiveness of the disinfection process by microbiological analysis of the water.
